How To Set Up Visual Studios:

1.Download Visual Studios From Google Chrome.

2.Create A Folder On Your Desktop.

3.Open Visual Studios And Click On File.

4.Select 'Open Folder' And Select The Folder You Created.

5.Right Click On The Left Side Of Visual Studios And Click On 'New File'.

6.Create Two Files Namely index.html And style.sss.

Follow The 3 Simple Steps To Open Console:

1.Right Click On The Website And Select Inspect Or Do Ctrl+Shift+I.

2.Once Done, You Will See A Popout. Click On The Console Button On The Top Of The Popout.

3.And There You Have The Console Screen.
